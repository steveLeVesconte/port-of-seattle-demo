Welcome to the Port of Seattle VendorConnect

Port of Seattle VendorConnect provides procurement and contract information for construction, consulting, and goods and services. This is also the one stop location for registering to be on the following rosters: small works (construction less than $300,000), consulting services, and goods and services.

To begin, you must register yourself and then find or add your business. If you were in our old system, and this is your first time logging in you must recreate your login account. To do this, click the “REGISTER/LOGIN” button below, then the “Sign up now” link on the login screen.

You may search our solicitations, vendors, and view the event calendar as a guest.
